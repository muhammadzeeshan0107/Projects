Our story

About Us

One on One Palace was founded in 2010 in Lahore. We use only fresh ingredients to serve authentic Italian food with a Pakistani twist.

From a family recipe to a local favorite

What started as a small family kitchen became a warm neighborhood restaurant built around hand-tossed dough, house-made sauces, and shared meals that feel memorable without being formal.

Our kitchen keeps the menu simple, seasonal, and generous so guests can enjoy comfort food with a fresh finish.

Why choose us

  • Fresh dough, sauces, and toppings prepared daily
  • Friendly service for families, friends, and celebrations
  • Balanced menu with vegetarian and meat options
  • Easy reservations and reliable takeaway support
